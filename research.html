Research Overview

My research focuses on the physical and chemical processes in interacting close binary systems and explosive stellar events, explored through multi-wavelength observations. I employ advanced spectral modeling tools, including the CLOUDY photoionization code to investigate the detailed ionization structure and chemical composition of nova ejecta, and the SHAPE morpho-kinematic code to reconstruct the three-dimensional morphology and dynamics of the ejecta during outburst.

Current Projects

  • Infrared and optical study of novae.
  • 3D Modeling of Nova eject using radiative transfer codes
